Who We Are

Exel Painting is a Toronto based painting company that specializes in residential and commercial painting. With years of experience in the industry, we offer fast, efficient, and quality work that exceeds expectations.

Are you looking for a painting company for your home or business project? Then look no further as Exel Painting offers:

  • Honesty and Transparency
  • Competitive pricing
  • Reliable customer service
  • Professional experienced team
  • Superior workmanship
  • 15 years of experience
  • Lifetime Warranty on interior jobs and 3 year warranty on exterior jobs

Exel Painting is dedicated to providing high quality painting services to homeowners and businesses. With several years of experience, our team of professionals have the expertise to handle all types of painting projects. Whether you are looking to refresh your home or get it ready to sell, we have the knowledge and skills to get the job done well.
