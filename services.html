Commercial Painting

Exel painting offers commercial painting that will work around your business needs to minimize disruptions to your clients and employees. We can work evening or overnight depending on your request. Our Commercial painting services include:

  • Restaurants

  • Hospitals and Medical Offices
  • Stores and Businesses
  • Hotels and Motels
  • Gyms

  • All other businesses

Interior Painting

Interior home painting is the easiest ways to make a big change for your home. Our Interior services include:

  • Full house painting
  • Condo painting
  • Basement painting
  • Garage painting
  • Drywall painting
  • Kitchen cabinet painting/spray painting
  • Popcorn/stucco ceiling removal and painting
  • Stair/landing sanding, painting, and varnishing

Our Process

Contact Us

The most important part of any job is the surface preparation. We make sure to clean and cover all surfaces. Next we patch and sand areas needed. Lastly we clean all the dust and apply a coat of primer.

Depending on the project, we use brush, roll, or spray paint. We use 2 coats of high quality Benjamin Moore, Sherwin William, Steels Paint, or Behr to make your house look like new.

After all the touch ups are done, we perform a full clean up of all areas.

Once you review our work, we will provide you with a review form to fill out as we are always looking to improve our processes.
